About Us

At HomeDot Care, we care about choice. Giving choice to our clients in all aspects of the homecare and support services we offer them. We know how important it is that our clients know that they have a choice in matters concerning their daily living and maintain a degree of independence in deciding how their care and support is carried out.

Our mission statement sums it up – to be the preferred quality homecare provider delivering exceptional care and support services to enhance the overall safety and wellbeing of the people we look after.

At HomeDot Care, our values of Choice, Respect, Honesty, Personal Impact and Reliability are key in all facets of our organisation’s delivery of services and we work with our clients, staff and stakeholders to ensure that at all points of engagement, these values are realised.

At HomeDot Care we are proud of the difference we make to the daily living of our clients. We are also proud of the staff we have in place who are engaged, dedicated and truly care about providing a unique service.

With over 25 years of management experience in the social care and care consultancy sector, we are confident that as a privately owned domiciliary care agency, we deliver on our promise on providing high quality home care services.

We provide domiciliary care in the community to everyone on a daily, weekly, overnight and home-live-in basis. Elderly care forms the core of the home care services we provide.

We also provide domiciliary care to people suffering with mental health conditions, learning difficulties, physical disabilities, medical conditions and dementia.

Our vision is what we do day to day as a business - to provide the person-centred care you would choose for a loved one!

Care Which Gives You A Choice

At HomeDot Care we work to provide high quality care and daily living assistance. Whether you need daily or weekly assistance due to ageing, illness, recovery, rehabilitation or just need that extra support, our care workers will provide a person-centred service that is reliable. All services provided by our care agency will offer you as the client choice in how you would like to see your care and support carried out.

Tailored Care Planning

HomeDot Care understands that not one care plan fits all. Daily services can include anything from meal preparation, personal care, cleaning, and shopping. Our home care services involves all aspects of daily living. We will take the time to get to know you and develop an individualized care plan that fits your specific needs.

Companionship

Companionship is key to a trusted relationship with our care workers. At HomeDot Care, we not only strive to help you with everyday tasks but want to develop a caring relationship with you so that you receive a holistic home care package.

Service

Domestic Services

Arthritis, physical disabilities and age related conditions are some of the conditions that may prevent our clients from being able to carry out their day to day domestic tasks as they normally would. All care workers of our care agency will work upon our clients' instructions and support them by carrying out an array of tasks such as doing the laundry, washing up, getting the grocery shopping, cleaning and other domestic activities.

Respite Services

We know the important role that family and other informal carers play in taking care of their loved ones. We also know how big this responsibility is and how when they take a break, they want the reassurance that whoever is taking care of their loved one will do so with the same level of care, dignity and empathy. At HomeDot Care our care workers will do just that and with a smile!

Personal Care

Our experienced and highly trained care workers will provide the highest quality of care to both the client and their families. The goal of our care agency is to help individuals and their loved ones live happy and fulfilling lives.
